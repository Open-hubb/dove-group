## About Us

Dove Group of Companies is a Sierra Leonean conglomerate founded in 2022, operating across five dynamic sectors. From renewable energy to foreign exchange, from fresh farm produce to building and construction, we are committed to delivering quality services and products that power communities and grow businesses across the region.

Our group brings together expertise in hospitality, communications, solar energy, building and construction, agriculture, and money remittances — making us a one-stop group of companies trusted by individuals and businesses alike.

---

## Our Subsidiaries

### 1. Dove Communications
Providing mobile handsets, smart TVs, and telecommunications products for connected living across Sierra Leone.

### 2. Dove Solar
Delivering solar generators and clean energy solutions for homes and businesses seeking reliable, sustainable power.

### 3. Dove Foreign Exchange Bureau
Offering reliable currency exchange and money remittance services, connecting Sierra Leone to the world.

### 4. Dove Farm
Producing and distributing palm oil, cooking oil, and agricultural produce for local consumption and export markets.

### 5. Dove Construction
Delivering professional building and construction services for residential and commercial projects.

### 6. Dove Petroleum
Supplying petroleum products and energy distribution to serve the growing local market.
