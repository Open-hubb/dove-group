Who We Are

Built for Africa's Growing Economy

Dove Group of Companies is a Sierra Leonean conglomerate founded in 2022, operating across six dynamic sectors. From renewable energy to foreign exchange, from fresh farm produce to building and construction, we are committed to delivering quality services and products that power communities and grow businesses across the region.

Our group brings together expertise in hospitality, communications, solar energy, building and construction, agriculture, and money remittances — making us a one-stop group of companies trusted by individuals and businesses alike.

Our Companies

Six Subsidiaries,
One Vision

Each subsidiary serves a critical sector of Sierra Leone's economy, united under the Dove Group's commitment to quality and community impact.
